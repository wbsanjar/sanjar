About TIT Bhopal

Established in 1997, Technocrats Institute of Technology (TIT) Bhopal is one of the premier engineering colleges in Madhya Pradesh, affiliated to Rajiv Gandhi Proudyogiki Vishwavidyalaya (RGPV).

Our institute offers UG and PG programs in various engineering disciplines with state-of-the-art infrastructure, experienced faculty, and excellent placement records.

Uniform Guidelines

Uniform Information

  • Uniform is compulsory for all students during college hours
  • Includes 2 pairs of shirts and trousers (boys) or kurtas (girls)
  • Purchase from authorized vendor in campus store
  • Uniform cost: ₹2,800 (included in first semester fees)
  • Replacement cost: ₹1,400 per set

Wearing Schedule

  • Monday to Friday: Full uniform mandatory
  • Saturday: Formal dress (college t-shirt with dark trousers)
  • Labs: Additional lab coat is required (provided separately)
